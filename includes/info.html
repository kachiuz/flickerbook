About Flickerbook

Online payslip generator and storage, working hours tracker, shift calendar... Flickerbook is all this and more in one place!

Designed for weekly PAYE employees and easy to use. Know your payments and deductions in just a few clicks of a button!

Flickerbook helps you keep a track of your earnings. It enables you to witness how your income, Tax, National Insurance and other payments and deductions change over time.

Calculating overtime, unsocial hours and pay, bank holiday payments, extra weekend pay, holiday pay, sick pay, paternal pay, compassionate pay, bereavement pay.. is made easy on Flickerbook! Read Our User Guide to see it for Yourself.

Flickerbook is also a calendar! It's very handy for those working on irregular shift patterns and hours, as it keeps a track of both!

All the features mentioned above are free of charge!

Premium Benefits

Flickerbook also offers a Premium membership, which grants access to even deeper insight to your earnings and deductions. After registration you will have access to Premium for one week free of charge!

Premium subscription costs 2.99£ per month. Alternatively you can access premium features for one week whenever You want and as often as you want for 0.99£ charge.

Premium provides additional statistics, such as year to date total payments received, hours and days worked, year to date weekly, daily, hourly averages, last 3 months weekly payments and deductions averages, pie and column charts.

Get access to features such as holidays tracker, back pay calculator, totals calculator and calendar generator.

Holidays tracker enables you to know how many holidays you have booked, used or left free to use. Also counts the days since your last and upcoming holidays.

Back pay calculation has never been easier! If all payslips have been generated and stored for period for which the back pay is due it can be calculated in a matter of seconds!

Want to know how much you earned in last month? 6 months? 9 months? 2 years? Totals calculator enables you to see your payments and deductions for your chosen period.

Add your shift pattern to calendar with Calendar Generator. It's easy to do no matter what your shift pattern is like!
